About Konini

Konini Agenturer is a fashion agency based in Stockholm founded by Edda Hallin in 2014.

With passion as a thrust we have created a portfolio of brands that offers stylish fashion with a twist. We want to deliver more than just a temporary trend, instead we want to deliver a feeling of uniqueness and a feeling of every day luxury. Same as for the women we address we want to offer something that not only look fantastic but that also comes with a story of its own.

Edda has been working in the fashion industry for more than 15 years and have a great knowledge of representing brands in the Scandinavian territory.

Besides from fashion Edda also have a passion for adventures and sports activities such as rock climbing and sea kayaking. This passion lead to a year in New Zealand and it was love at first sight. While in New Zealand Edda stayed in a house on Konini Street, a beautiful house surrounded by lovely fuchsia colored Konini flowers.

Long-term relationships, good service and personal attention is our key to success.

Konini Agenturer have it’s base in Stockholm and our customers are located in Sweden, Finland, Norway, Denmark and Iceland. We have a third party warehouse in Sweden that handles stock and deliveries. Filippa Smedhagen handles our PR.
